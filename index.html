We have a great future ahead

We have a better future for orangutans and the rainforest ahead. It’s a future where we can buy products without ruining animal habitats because of unsustainable palm oil. In our future, living a sustainable life will be as easy as looking out for the Safety Ape stamp.

We need to fix palm oil – fast

We need to get to that future. Right now, unsustainable palm oil is everywhere. 2.5 rugby fields of rainforest a cleared every minute to produce unsustainable palm oil. 90% of the orangutan’s habitat has been lost to palm oil already. But the Safety Ape stamp is helping more and more people say no to the status quo.

Look out for the Stamp

When you see the Safety Ape Stamp, you can be guaranteed that the palm oil is sustainable. Whether it is ice cream or lipstick, Safety Ape approved products protect the forest, the orangutan and our climate. If we only buy Safety Ape approved, we will cut demand for bad palm oil. The more of us commit to buying sustainably, the better the planet gets. Simple.
